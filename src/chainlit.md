# Operation: Deep Atlas

*Clearance Level: Eyes Only*

Welcome, Agent. You have been selected for a high-priority covert operation. The Taskmaster will guide you through each phase of your mission — but stay sharp. One wrong move and the operation is burned.

## Mission Phases

| Phase              | Objective                                                         |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Travel**         | Receive your destination. Memorise it. Tell no one.               |
| **Disguise**       | Check local weather conditions and choose your cover accordingly. |
| **Crack the Code** | Decrypt the intercepted cipher using your gadget.                 |
| **Complete**       | Report back to the Taskmaster with your findings.                 |

## Available Gadgets

- **Weather** — Query real-time conditions for any city
- **Decrypt** — Break Caesar-cipher encoded transmissions

## Instructions

1. Click **New Game** to receive your mission briefing from the Taskmaster.
2. Follow the Taskmaster's instructions through each phase.
3. Use your gadgets when prompted — the Taskmaster will call them for you.
4. Complete all phases to close the mission.

---

*Good luck, Agent. The Taskmaster is watching.*
